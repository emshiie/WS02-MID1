About Blossom

Blossom is the leader of the Powerpuff Girls. Like her fiery red hair, Blossom is strong and determined, but she's also methodical and thinks things through. Blossom doesn't run into a situation with just her emotions ruling her.

Blossom's Characteristics

  • Blossom is strong and determined, but she's also methodical and thinks things through.
  • Blossom doesn't run into a situation with just her emotions ruling her.
  • She gives insightful advice and goes to great lengths to ensure that people close to her are happy.
